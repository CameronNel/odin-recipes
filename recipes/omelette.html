Omelette

Description

An omelette is an egg dish with a filling of choice

An omelette is the perfect choice for a versatile breakfast

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Milk
  • Filling of choice

Steps

  1. Beat eggs with milk and pour onto pan
  2. Add filling of choice on top of mixture
  3. Flip into a half moon shape
  4. Enjoy!
