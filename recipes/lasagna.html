Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a comfort food enjoyed by many

Lasagna can be made with pasta sheets or shells

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Cheese of choice
  • Pasta sheets
  • Lasagna sauce

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in a pan
  2. In a baking tray, layer pasta sheets and beef
  3. Cover with pasta sauce
  4. Put in an oven at 200C for 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
